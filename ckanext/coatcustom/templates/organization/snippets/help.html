The monitoring activity in COAT is organized into food web modules. Each of the food web modules includes a subsets of strongly linked species (or functional groups/species assemblages) that have high climate sensitivity and key functions. Key functions can be either ecological and/or societal. Each module is named by one of itâ€™s primary monitoring targets - often the one with the highest management relevance.

Within modules, admins can assign roles and authorise its members, giving individual users the right to publish datasets from that particular module .
